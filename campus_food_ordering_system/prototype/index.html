Help & Support

How do I place an order? expand_more
Browse the menu, add items to your cart, go to checkout, enter your location, and place the order. You'll receive a WhatsApp notification when it's ready!
How do I pick up my order? expand_more
When your order is ready, visit Fresco's Pizza store. Pay cash at the counter and slide the accept button on your phone to confirm. Choose "Self Pickup" to take away or "Dine-in" to eat at the store.
Can I cancel my order? expand_more
Yes, you can cancel your order before it reaches "Ready for Pickup" status. Go to Orders → Track → Cancel Order.
What payment methods are available? expand_more
We accept Cash at Store (for Dine-in & Pickup), UPI to Delivery Agent (for Delivery), and Online Payment via Razorpay (UPI, Credit/Debit Card, Net Banking). All orders include GST-compliant invoices.
